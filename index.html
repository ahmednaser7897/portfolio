Who I Am

A brief introduction about my journey as a developer

I'm a Mid-level Flutter Developer based in Cairo, Egypt, with a passion for building user-centric mobile applications. With a BSc in Computer Science from Helwan University and 3+ years of hands-on experience, I specialize in creating scalable, high-performance cross-platform apps.
